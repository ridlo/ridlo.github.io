Hello.

My name is Ridlo W. Wibowo and I am an astrophysicist (wannabe). I am currently a PhD student at Astronomy Department, Institut Teknologi Bandung. My research interests are broad. They include minor-planet (asteroids), planet formation, extrasolar planet, new moon observation, radio telescope, active galactic nuclei, rotational spectra of molecules, N-body simulation, hydrodynamic simulation and high performance computing.
