Hello!

My name is Ridlo W. Wibowo, and I am currently a lecturer at the Department of Atmospheric and Planetary Sciences, Institut Teknologi Sumatera. My research interests are very broad. They include minor-planet (asteroids), planet formation, extrasolar planet, new moon observation, radio/submm observation, active galactic nuclei, N-body simulation, hydrodynamic simulation, high performance computing, big data analysis and machine learning. I also have interest in Math e.g. prime number.
